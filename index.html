Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions you’d like answered please feel free to email us.

What is a Bookmark?

A bookmark is a saved link to a website that you can easily access later. With our extension, you can quickly access your favorite sites from any device, as your bookmarks sync automatically.

How can I request a new browser?

If you’d like to request support for a new browser, please contact our support team through the "Contact Us" page. We’re always open to feedback and will consider adding more browsers based on demand.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes, we offer a mobile app that allows you to access and sync your bookmarks on the go. It’s available for both iOS and Android devices.

What about other Chromium browsers?

Our extension is compatible with most Chromium-based browsers. If you’re using a Chromium browser, there’s a good chance our extension will work seamlessly.
